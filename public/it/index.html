Les Houches

Peaceful base for big mountain days

Set in the Les Hauts de Chavants residence, Apartment Tête Rousse has direct piste access from the garden, with the Prarion lift just two minutes downhill on foot.

Below the apartment, Lac de Chavants changes with the seasons. In winter, it is home to the ESF Jardin des Enfants nursery ski area. In summer, the lake becomes a relaxed outdoor hub, with rock climbing, fishing, tennis, a children’s play area, a café and treetop adventure activities close by.
